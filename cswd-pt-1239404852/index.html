In addition to the marks awarded for the CAs, the final grade will also include 10% for lab work (5 labs, 2% each).

  • The deadline for lab uploads is the starting time of the following session (e.g. if a lab assignment was given at session 1 then it is due at 6pm on the day of session 2).
