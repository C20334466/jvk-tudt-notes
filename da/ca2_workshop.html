Mini-Workshop: The Analysis Process

  1. Ask a question that can be answered by analysing data
    • "What is 2+2?" - does not require analysis to be answered (deterministic solution)
    • "What is the weather like today?" - valid but trivial (the single data point can be collected by looking out the window)
    • "Why am I always hungry?" - valid
      • would require a lot of personal data collection
      • may not meet criteria for CA/project
    • "What is the average length of a pop song?" - valid
      • answering it would involve a a good bit of data preparation
      • would require basic knowledge of statistics but may not meet CA/project criteria because the analysis part is not complex enough
    • "Is there a relationship between the average age of actors in a film and its popularity?" - valid
    • "Can the detection of troll-posted social media messages be automated?" - valid
    • "What causes homelessness?" - valid
  2. Look for data that can help in answering the question
    • looking at data might inspire further questions or even the main question
    • more than one source is often needed
